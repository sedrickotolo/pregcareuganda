About Pregcare

Pregcare is an IoT device that helps Health workers remotely monitor health vitals of Pregnant mothers in real time

Monitring you for safety is our pride.

Pregcare

With Pregcare device, we take real time monitoring of Pregnant mothers througth our scalable wearable Device. The Device updates the medical personels on current body temperatue, heart beats, GPS location and real time SMS of body status. 

Our device is designed and usable for poor mothers in remote villages where there is no internet connections. 

body temperature

The average Body temperature is 37 Celcius. Our device reads your temperature values and analysis it when there is a drastic change

heart beats

The average heart beat is 72 in adults and it varies in adults and kids. Real time monitoring of the Heart Beats is effected for proper analysis.

GPS location

Your location is monitored for reach out incase of accidents, emergencies or any other related.
