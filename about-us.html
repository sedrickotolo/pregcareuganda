The Special Pregcare Device Development

Problem

Uganda is presently experiencing a demographic move to the extent that most of the pregnant mothers lose their lives during and before birth. They (mothers) also experience problems of poor monitoring of their health which leads to many complications in the country. This causes so many complications and death in mothers due to poor monitoring system before birth.

Solution Birthing Pregcare

Solution

Hence we develop a wearable Internet of Things (IoT) device for the pregnant mothers which will evaluate their health condition by constantly monitoring heart rate, body temperature, pressure and location using GPS sensor and will send these data to a web application using a Lora Gateway for Real time monitoring by Doctors. The Device doesn't need Internet connection since we aim at saving lives to the poor mothers as well.
